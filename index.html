About Daringbadi

Known as the "Kashmir of Odisha", Daringbadi is a serene hill station that captivates visitors with its natural beauty, rich tribal heritage, and cool mountain climate.

Daringbadi, known as the “Kashmir of Odisha”, is a beautiful hill station in Kandhamal district. Surrounded by pine forests, coffee and spice plantations, and cool weather, it is one of the few places in Odisha that sometimes sees snowfall. With its natural beauty, rich tribal culture, and peaceful atmosphere, Daringbadi is a perfect place to experience both nature and tradition.

The village is also home to warm and friendly people who preserve their unique culture and traditions. Visitors can enjoy local hospitality, explore scenic spots, and experience the simple charm of village life.
